About Furniwell Indonesia

Indonesian furniture production rooted in Semarang and Jepara.

Furniwell Indonesia manufactures solid-wood furniture, handicrafts, and home accessories for project, wholesale, and export requirements.

Built around practical buyer requirements.

Furniwell works with product references and buyer specifications covering dimensions, materials, finish, hardware, packing, and shipment preparation.

The company’s original website archive includes production, shipment, and international project references. This redesigned version surfaces that evidence in a clearer structure instead of hiding it inside old category pages.

Business information must remain consistent across the website, Google Business Profile, social media, and future merchant accounts. The office, showroom, and production addresses are therefore labelled by function rather than mixed together.
